# Sales & Revenue Dashboard 2024

## Project Overview
An interactive Sales & Revenue Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI Desktop.

## Features
- 4 KPI Cards — Total Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value, Units Sold
- Monthly Revenue Trend — Line chart showing growth across 2024
- Revenue by Category — Donut chart breaking down Electronics, Apparel, Food & Bev, Home & Garden
- Top Products by Revenue — Ranked horizontal bar chart
- Interactive Slicers — Filter by Region and Category instantly

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel / CSV

## Key Insights
- Electronics contributes 70% of total revenue
- Smart TV 55" is the top performing product
- Revenue steadily increases towards year-end (holiday season effect)
- Total revenue of $101K across 120 transactions in 2024

1. Download the Sales_Data.pbix file

3. Use the Region and Category slicers to filter the data interactively
